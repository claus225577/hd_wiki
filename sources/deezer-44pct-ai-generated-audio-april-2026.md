# Deezer: 44% of Songs Uploaded Daily Are AI-Generated

## Key Facts
- **44% of songs** uploaded daily to Deezer are AI-generated
- **85% of AI-music streams** flagged as fraudulent (artificial streaming inflation)
- Demonstrates the scale of generative audio AI capabilities in 2026

## Hearing AI Relevance
- Generative audio AI shares technology roots with speech enhancement for hearing aids:
  - Both use neural networks to model and generate/modify audio signals
  - Audio generation models (diffusion, GANs, flow-matching) are architecturally related to speech enhancement models
  - Advances in generative audio (naturalness, real-time synthesis) feed back into speech reconstruction quality
- The 44% figure demonstrates that neural audio generation has reached production scale and quality
- Potential hearing aid applications of generative audio tech:
  - Speech reconstruction from degraded signals (generative enhancement vs. traditional masking)
  - Personalized audio rendering (generating optimized audio for individual hearing profiles)
  - Synthetic training data generation for hearing aid DNN training
