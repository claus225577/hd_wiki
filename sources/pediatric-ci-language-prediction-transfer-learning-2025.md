# Deep Transfer Learning for Pediatric CI Language Outcome Prediction

## Summary

Multicenter cohort study of 278 pediatric CI recipients spanning 3 languages. Uses bilinear attention-based deep transfer learning model on pre-implant MRI to forecast spoken language improvement. Achieves 92.39% accuracy.

## Key Contributions

- 278 pediatric CI recipients across 3 languages (multicenter, multilingual)
- Pre-implant MRI as input — predicts outcomes BEFORE surgery
- Bilinear attention-based deep transfer learning architecture
- 92.39% accuracy in forecasting spoken language improvement
- Could guide surgical decisions and parental counseling

## Significance

For families deciding whether to pursue CI surgery for their child, this model could provide evidence-based predictions of language outcomes based on pre-operative brain imaging. The multilingual validation (3 languages) demonstrates generalizability beyond a single language/culture.
