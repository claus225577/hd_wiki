# Starkey Omega AI — AAA 2026 Showcase

## Key Facts

- **Event:** AAA 2026 Conference (April 22-25, San Antonio)
- **Platform:** Omega AI — Starkey's latest flagship
- **Key features showcased:**
  - Auracast broadcast audio reception
  - AI-driven sound processing
  - Health and wellness integration (fall detection, activity tracking, respiratory rate, heart rate)

## Extraction

Starkey used AAA 2026 to showcase the full Omega AI platform capabilities, highlighting three pillars: Auracast connectivity (aligning with the industry-wide LE Audio rollout), AI-driven sound processing (leveraging the G3 Gen AI Neuro Processor with integrated NPU), and health/wellness integration (Starkey's key differentiator with fall detection, activity tracking, and respiratory rate monitoring). The AAA showcase confirms Starkey's continued positioning as a health-tech company rather than a pure hearing aid manufacturer.
