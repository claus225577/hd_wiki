# Starkey Genesis AI — Fall Detection and Health Tracking

## Key Extractions
- Launched February 2023
- First hearing aid with integrated fall detection using 3D motion sensors
- Health and fitness tracking covers walks, runs, biking, and aerobic activity
- Activity recognition runs on-device using AI classifiers
- Hear Share app enables sharing of health data with caregivers or family
- 51-hour battery life across the product line

## Relevance
Genesis AI is the reference case for hearing aids as health platforms beyond audiology. Fall detection via 3D sensors in an ear-worn device is clinically significant — the ear is a stable mounting point compared to wrist wearables. The health tracking features position Starkey in competition with general-purpose wearables, a strategic direction likely to intensify as the OTC market grows.
