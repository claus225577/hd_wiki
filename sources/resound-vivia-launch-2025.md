## Key Facts

- ReSound Vivia launched February 2025 — first ReSound hearing aid with an integrated DNN processor.
- Dual-chip architecture: traditional 360 signal processor combined with a dedicated DNN inference chip.
- DNN trained on 13.5M sentences with 3.9M parameters; performs approximately 4.9 trillion operations per day during normal use.
- World's smallest AI micro-RIE (Receiver-In-Ear) form factor at launch.
- Native Bluetooth LE Audio support with Auracast broadcast audio reception — enabling direct venue audio streaming to hearing aids.
- 64% of users in study reported improved hearing in noise vs. previous generation device.
- GN Audio (parent company) positioned Vivia to compete directly with Phonak Sphere Infinio in the AI hearing aid segment, approximately 6 months after Phonak's launch.
