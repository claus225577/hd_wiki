# Amplifon Acquires GN Hearing for $2.6B

Amplifon announced the acquisition of GN Hearing (ReSound) for approximately $2.6 billion, making it the largest hearing aid industry deal in a decade.

## Key Facts
- Deal value: ~$2.6B USD
- Buyer: Amplifon (world's largest hearing aid retailer, 10,000+ clinics, 100+ countries)
- Target: GN Hearing (manufacturer of ReSound hearing aids, part of GN Group)
- This merges retail distribution with manufacturing — a vertical integration play

## Data Science Significance
- Combines two previously separate datasets: retail patient journey data + manufacturer product telemetry and R&D data
- Creates a closed-loop data flywheel: audiologist fitting decisions + long-term patient outcomes + real-world device usage patterns + product performance feeding back into R&D
- Comparable to Netflix's transition from content distributor to content producer using viewing data

## Industry Implications
- May trigger a wave of vertical integration across the hearing industry
- Other retailers (Audika, HearingLife/Demant) may seek manufacturing partnerships
- Manufacturers without retail presence (Starkey, WS Audiology) face strategic questions
