# Amplifon Acquiring GN Hearing for €2.3B

## Key Extractions
- Amplifon (retail audiology network) acquiring GN Hearing (ReSound, Beltone, Jabra Enhance manufacturer) for €2.3B (~$2.64B USD)
- GN receives €1.69B in cash plus 56 million Amplifon shares
- Creates a vertically integrated retail + manufacturing player
- Combined revenue of approximately €3.3B
- Combined workforce exceeds 20,000 employees across 100+ countries
- Expected synergies of €60–80M annually by 2029
- Transaction expected to close by end of 2026

## Relevance
This is the largest hearing industry consolidation in years and the first major vertical integration combining a top-5 manufacturer with the world's largest retail audiology network. If completed, it structurally changes how ReSound/Beltone products reach consumers and raises competitive pressure on Sonova, Demant, and WS Audiology. It also creates a powerful data flywheel — Amplifon's retail patient data combined with GN's product development.
