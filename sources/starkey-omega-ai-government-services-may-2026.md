# Starkey — Omega AI Reaches Government Services (May 1, 2026)

## Key Extraction

- **What:** Starkey announced its latest Omega AI advancements are now available to U.S. Government Services audiologists — VA (Veterans Affairs), DoD (Department of Defense), and IHS (Indian Health Service) — as of May 1, 2026.
- **Feature set delivered:**
  - Auracast Assistant (broadcast-audio discovery via the Starkey app)
  - Push-to-Talk
  - Google Fast Pair (single-tap Android pairing)
  - **StarLink Edge LE Adapter** — a new accessory enabling LE Audio connectivity to legacy/non-LE source devices, important for VA/DoD environments where source-side hardware is mixed-vintage
- **Hardening claims:** Waterproofing 10× stronger than previous coatings; custom in-ear products engineered up to 20× tougher — both meaningful in active-duty settings.

## Why This Matters

- **Channel rather than product news.** The features themselves rolled out broadly in January 2026 (see [[../sources/starkey-omega-ai-auracast-update-jan-2026]]); the news is that the Government Services portfolio — historically slow to refresh — now has parity. The VA alone fits ~700,000 hearing aids per year, so this materially expands Omega AI's installed base.
- **StarLink Edge LE Adapter** is the genuinely new artifact: it makes the bridge to LE Audio a stand-alone accessory rather than a phone-dependent feature, important for environments without consumer smartphones.
- **Strengthens Starkey's positioning as the "hardened-environment" OEM** vis-à-vis Oticon (Verit/Intent) and Phonak (Infinio) in the Federal channel.
