# Edge AI Audio Maturation — Embedded World 2026

## Key Extraction

- **Synaptics Astra SR80** — new chip targeting "hearable intelligence"; dedicated edge AI processing for audio devices
- **AONDevices** — customizable edge AI speech enhancement platform; always-on neural processing
- **Aizip** — integration with **PYOUR Audio framework** for tiny AI in hearing devices
- Coverage of Embedded World 2026 conference showing edge AI audio moving from demo to production

## Key Players and Products

### Synaptics Astra SR80
- Dedicated chip for **hearable intelligence** — targeting the convergence of consumer earbuds and hearing devices
- Edge AI processing optimized for audio applications (speech enhancement, noise reduction, voice commands)
- Synaptics is a major embedded AI chip vendor (acquired Conexant, DSP Group) — their entry into hearable-specific silicon signals market maturity

### AONDevices
- **Always-on neural processing** — ultra-low-power AI that runs continuously without draining battery
- **Customizable speech enhancement** — OEMs can tailor the AI model to their specific product requirements
- Targets both consumer earbuds and hearing devices

### Aizip + PYOUR Audio
- **Aizip** provides the tiny AI inference engine (see also: `aizip-tiny-ai-hearing-devices-2026.md`)
- **PYOUR Audio** provides the audio processing framework
- Integration enables AI-enhanced audio on standard microcontrollers without dedicated NPUs
- Relevant to OTC hearing aids and lower-tier devices that lack custom silicon

## Significance

- **Embedded World 2026** is the premier embedded systems conference — edge AI audio having prominent presence signals the technology is moving from research to production
- Multiple vendors offering hearable AI chips/platforms indicates supply chain maturation — hearing device OEMs now have multiple sourcing options for edge AI silicon
- **Synaptics entry** is notable: a major semiconductor company (not a startup) investing in hearable-specific AI silicon
- The combination of Synaptics (large vendor), AONDevices (specialized startup), and Aizip (TinyML platform) represents a maturing ecosystem at all levels

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- **Supply chain diversification:** Big 6 hearing aid companies currently design custom silicon; these vendors could enable smaller OEMs to ship AI-capable hearing devices
- **OTC enablement:** Platforms like Aizip + PYOUR Audio lower the barrier to AI-enhanced OTC hearing aids
- **Hearable intelligence as a category:** Synaptics naming their chip for "hearable intelligence" validates the convergence of consumer audio and hearing health
- **Always-on AI** (AONDevices) is critical for hearing devices — must process continuously, not on-demand
- Competition from general-purpose edge AI chips could eventually pressure Big 6 to open their platforms or face silicon commoditization
