# ReSound Vivia — First ReSound DNN Hearing Aid

## Key Extractions
- Launched February 2025 at CES
- First ReSound hearing aid to feature a dedicated DNN chip
- DNN trained on 13.5 million spoken sentences
- Performs 4.9 trillion operations per day
- Intelligent Focus feature adapts sound directionality based on head direction
- First hearing aid to be fully Auracast-equipped at launch

## Relevance
Vivia marks GN's entry into dedicated DNN silicon for hearing aids, aligning with the broader industry shift toward purpose-built AI chips. The Auracast integration is a significant interoperability milestone. Training on spoken sentences (rather than general sound scenes) reflects a speech-first design philosophy worth tracking against competitors.
