# Bluetooth LE Audio Auracast Rollout — 2025-2026

## Key Extractions
- 2025 marked the transition of Auracast from promise to real-world deployment
- Venues deploying Auracast: airports, theaters, universities, fitness centers
- 50% of hearing aids projected to be Auracast-compatible by 2026
- Full market penetration projected by 2030
- Auracast replaces aging induction loop and FM-based assistive listening systems
- Hybrid rollout strategy recommended: maintain legacy systems during transition period
- Benefit over legacy systems: lossless audio, no interference zones, multiple simultaneous channels, smartphone accessibility without dedicated receiver
- Enables hearing aid wearers to stream venue audio directly to devices without intermediary hardware

## Relevance
Auracast is the infrastructure layer that transforms hearing aids from personal devices into network-connected endpoints. The 2025-2026 deployment wave sets the stage for ambient audio accessibility at scale — a fundamental shift in public hearing accommodation.
