# Predicting Cognitive Outcomes from Hearing Aid Use

## Summary

Develops a predictive model to identify which hearing aid users will experience cognitive benefits from hearing intervention. Published in Frontiers in Aging Neuroscience, 2025.

## Key Contributions

- Prediction model for identifying patients where hearing aids are most beneficial for cognition
- Addresses the ACHIEVE trial finding that not all hearing-impaired adults benefit equally
- Moves toward precision audiology — targeting intervention to those who will benefit most

## Significance

Bridges AI/ML and clinical audiology for precision medicine. Rather than treating all hearing loss equally, predictive models can guide clinicians on which patients to prioritize for early intervention based on cognitive risk profiles.
