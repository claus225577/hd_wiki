# ML for Meniere's Disease Diagnosis from Audiometry

## Summary

Develops ML model to diagnose Meniere's disease from pure-tone audiometry features. Also predicts subtypes of endolymphatic hydrops. Published in Otolaryngology–Head and Neck Surgery, 2025.

## Key Contributions

- ML diagnosis of Meniere's disease from standard audiometric data
- Predicts endolymphatic hydrops subtypes
- Uses readily available clinical data (pure-tone audiometry) rather than expensive imaging
- Potential screening tool for Meniere's in primary care

## Significance

Meniere's disease is notoriously difficult to diagnose. An ML model that can flag probable Meniere's from routine audiometry could accelerate referral and treatment, reducing the diagnostic odyssey many patients experience.
