# AAA 2026 Annual Convention

## Key Facts

- **Theme:** "Amplify Your Value"
- **Dates:** April 22-24, 2026
- **Location:** San Antonio, Texas
- **Organizer:** American Academy of Audiology (AAA)
- **Major exhibitors:** Oticon, WS Audiology, Phonak, ReSound, Starkey, Cochlear
- **HearTECH Hub** — dedicated technology showcase area within the expo hall

## Product Launches at AAA 2026

- **Oticon Zeal** — rechargeable ITE with 2nd-gen AI, encapsulation tech, Auracast (debut)
- **Oticon Verit** — premium adult hearing aid (showcased, launched earlier in Apr 2026)
- **Oticon Play SI** — pediatric hearing aid (showcased, launched earlier in Apr 2026)
- **WS Audiology Sound Preference Program** — global rollout initiated Apr 20, showcased at AAA
- Additional launches/announcements from other exhibitors TBD

## Significance

- AAA is the largest US audiology conference — the primary venue for product launches and clinical research dissemination
- 2026 conference notable for the density of AI-powered product launches (Oticon alone debuting 3 AI-based products)
- **HearTECH Hub** signals AAA's recognition that technology is central to modern audiology, not ancillary
- All Big 6 manufacturers (Demant/Oticon, WSA/Signia, Sonova/Phonak, GN/ReSound, Starkey, Cochlear) exhibiting — full industry representation
- Theme "Amplify Your Value" reflects the profession's response to OTC disruption — audiologists demonstrating irreplaceable expertise beyond device dispensing

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- Primary industry event for hearing aid AI product launches and clinical evidence presentations
- AAA presentations increasingly feature AI/ML research sessions alongside traditional clinical topics
- HearTECH Hub creates a dedicated space for technology demonstrations, including AI features
