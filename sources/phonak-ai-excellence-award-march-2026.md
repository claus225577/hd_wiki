# Phonak AI Excellence Award (March 2026)

**Date:** March 24, 2026

## Key Details

- **Phonak Virto R Infinio** and **Infinio Ultra Sphere** won the **2026 AI Excellence Award**
- **Infinio Ultra Sphere** uses DNN for **direct speech/noise separation** — not just noise estimation, but active separation of speech from noise signals
- Award validates Sonova's DEEPSONIC dual-chip platform as recognized AI innovation
- Follows Starkey's two BIG AI Excellence Awards for Omega AI — both major US manufacturers now have AI award recognition in 2026

## Significance for Hearing AI

- **DNN for direct speech/noise separation:** The Ultra Sphere's approach of directly separating speech from noise (rather than estimating and subtracting noise) represents a more sophisticated signal processing paradigm
- **Award recognition trend:** Multiple hearing aid products winning AI awards in 2026 (Phonak + Starkey) signals that hearing aid AI is being recognized by the broader tech industry, not just audiology
- **Virto R Infinio win is notable:** This is a custom ITE/ITC form factor product — demonstrating that DEEPSONIC AI can be recognized even in space-constrained custom devices
- Strengthens Sonova's clinical and marketing narrative for the DEEPSONIC platform
