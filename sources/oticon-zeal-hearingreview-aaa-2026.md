# Oticon Zeal — Hearing Review Coverage (AAA 2026)

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Oticon Zeal™ — rechargeable in-the-ear (ITE) hearing aid
- **Debuted at:** AAA 2026 conference (Apr 22-24, San Antonio, TX)
- **Encapsulation technology** — novel manufacturing/sealing approach for ITE form factor durability and reliability
- **2nd-generation AI sound processing** — same generation as Oticon Verit and Play SI
- **LE Audio / Auracast connectivity** — confirms Auracast support, extending Oticon's full-lineup Auracast push to ITE
- **Rechargeable** — addresses the long-standing gap of rechargeable ITE hearing aids with modern connectivity

## Significance

- Completes Oticon's spring 2026 product blitz: Verit (premium adult), Play SI (pediatric), and Zeal (ITE) — all on 2nd-gen AI with Auracast
- Encapsulation technology addresses a key ITE challenge: moisture/cerumen ingress that degrades components in the ear canal
- Confirms every new Oticon product in 2026 ships with LE Audio/Auracast — Demant has committed fully to the standard
- ITE with 2nd-gen AI and Auracast represents the most technologically advanced ITE hearing aid to date

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- ITE form factor imposes tighter power/space constraints than BTE/RIC — running 2nd-gen AI inference in ITE demonstrates continued model compression advances
- Full Auracast across all form factors (BTE, RIC, ITE, pediatric) creates a unified connectivity ecosystem
- Encapsulation tech may enable more reliable sensor integration in ITE devices in the future
