## Key Facts

- Global hearing aids market projected to reach $8.55B by 2029, growing at 4.25% CAGR from 2025 baseline.
- Top 5 OEMs (Sonova, WS Audiology, Demant, GN Store Nord, Starkey) hold 92.4% combined market share — an extremely concentrated oligopoly.
- Key growth drivers: AI-powered signal processing, Bluetooth LE Audio/Auracast connectivity, rechargeable battery adoption, teleaudiology service expansion.
- OTC market segment emerging as a structural disruptor to the traditional prescription+dispensing model, though premium segment remains dominated by the top 5.
- Rechargeable hearing aids now the dominant form factor in premium tier — removes battery management barrier for older users.
- AI processing chip arms race (Phonak DEEPSONIC 2024, Starkey G3 NPU 2025) expected to accelerate R&D spend and shorten product cycles.
- Teleaudiology growth reducing distribution cost and extending geographic reach, particularly in Asia-Pacific and underserved US rural markets.
- Aging global population demographic tailwind sustains baseline demand growth independent of technology innovation cycles.
