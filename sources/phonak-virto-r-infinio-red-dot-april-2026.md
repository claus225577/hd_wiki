# Phonak Virto R Infinio — 2026 Red Dot Award

## Key Extractions

### Announcement
- On **29 April 2026**, Phonak (Sonova) announced its **Virto R Infinio** custom in-the-ear hearing aid received the 2026 Red Dot Award for Product Design.
- This is the second major industrial-design award for Virto R Infinio in 2026, following its earlier 2026 iF DESIGN AWARD.

### Product Context
- Virto R Infinio sits on Sonova's **Infinio platform**, which features:
  - DeepSonic chip with on-device DNN-driven noise reduction (SmartSpeech / Sphere)
  - Auracast / Bluetooth LE Audio support
  - Custom in-the-ear form factor (Virto family)
- Infinio Ultra Sphere previously won the 2026 iF DESIGN AWARD.

## Relevance
- A marketing milestone rather than a technical inflection — but worth tracking as Infinio consolidates its position as Sonova's flagship AI noise-reduction platform.
- Reinforces that custom in-the-ear remains a viable form factor for on-device DNN inference, alongside RIC.
- Continues a 2026 pattern of design awards stacking up for AI-driven hearing aids (see also Phonak 2026 AI Excellence Award, Starkey BIG AI awards).
