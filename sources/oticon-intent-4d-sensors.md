# Oticon Intent — World's First 4D Sensor Hearing Aid

## Key Extractions
- World's first hearing aid to use 4D sensors: combines head motion, body motion, conversation activity, and acoustic environment signals
- DNN 2.0 trained on 13 million sound scenes
- BrainHearing technology framework underpins signal processing philosophy
- miniBTE R form factor released November 2025
- Rated #1 prescription hearing aid in 2026

## Relevance
The 4D sensor fusion approach is architecturally distinct from competitors — most DNNs operate on acoustic input alone. Incorporating IMU-derived motion and conversation activity as DNN inputs represents a multimodal AI strategy that could set a template for future devices. Worth tracking how this sensor fusion affects classification accuracy in noisy environments.
