# Phonak Infinio Ultra Sphere & Virto R Infinio Win 2026 iF Design Award

## Summary
Phonak's **Infinio Ultra Sphere** and **Virto R Infinio** hearing aids received the 2026 **iF DESIGN AWARD** from iF International Forum Design.

- **Virto R Infinio**: combines Phonak's Infinio chip platform, universal connectivity, and a **precision custom-molded fit**, delivered as a **rechargeable** in-the-ear.
- **Infinio Ultra Sphere**: positioned at the top of the Infinio lineup with full DNN-driven Sphere noise-and-speech processing.

## Why It Matters
- Custom + rechargeable in-the-ear is technically hard: the form factor leaves almost no room for a battery cell, so every mm of acoustic, sensor, and battery layout is contested. Winning a design award signals the engineering integration is mature.
- Reinforces Sonova/Phonak's lead in the **rechargeable custom** segment, where Signia (Insio Charge&Go CIC IX, also new in April 2026) is the most direct competitor.
- Consumer-grade design recognition matters for OTC-adjacent positioning where industrial design competes with AirPods.
