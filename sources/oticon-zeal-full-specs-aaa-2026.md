# Oticon Zeal — Full Specs and Launch Details

## Key Facts

- **Positioning:** World's most discreet, complete hearing aid — nearly invisible ITE
- **AI:** DNN 2.0 always-on AI on Sirius chip (same as flagship Oticon Intent)
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth LE Audio + Auracast-ready
- **Battery:** 20-hour battery life, quick-charge (15 min = 4 hours)
- **Fitting:** Instant-fit option + micro-earmold custom option
- **Controls:** Tap controls via motion sensor
- **Fitting range:** Rated up to ~75 dB hearing loss
- **Launch venue:** AAA 2026 (April 22-25, San Antonio)

## Extraction

The Oticon Zeal represents the most technically advanced ITE hearing aid to date. Key distinction from prior reporting: the press release confirms DNN 2.0 (not just "2nd-generation AI") running on the Sirius chip — the same silicon powering the flagship Intent. This means the ITE form factor now has parity with the BTE/RIC flagship in terms of AI processing, which is notable given the tighter thermal and power constraints of an in-ear design. The 20-hour battery with 15-min quick-charge addresses the historical battery anxiety of rechargeable ITEs. The instant-fit option lowers the fitting barrier, potentially enabling same-day dispensing. The ~75 dB fitting range covers mild to moderately severe hearing loss, making this a viable option for the majority of hearing aid candidates. Tap controls via motion sensor eliminate the need for physical buttons, contributing to the discreet form factor.
