## Key Facts

- Launched October 2025 by Starkey.
- Powered by G3 Gen AI Neuro Processor, featuring a dedicated Neural Processing Unit (NPU) for on-device AI inference.
- DNN 360 — first DNN-powered directionality system in a hearing aid, enabling fully neural-network-driven spatial processing rather than traditional beamforming.
- 28% improvement in speech intelligibility vs. predecessor in tested conditions.
- 8 dB SNR improvement — one of the largest reported SNR gains in a commercial hearing aid.
- 51-hour battery life (with charging case) — among the longest in the premium hearing aid segment.
- Health features include respiratory rate tracking via in-ear microphone analysis.
- LED find-my feature for locating lost devices.
- Positions Starkey as the first US-headquartered major OEM to deploy a dedicated NPU in a hearing aid (following Phonak's DEEPSONIC chip, Aug 2024).
