## Key Facts

- Deep transfer learning model achieved 92% accuracy predicting spoken language outcomes 1–3 years post-cochlear implantation.
- Model trained on pre-implantation brain MRI data from 278 children spanning 3 languages (English, Mandarin, Spanish), making it one of the largest and most diverse pediatric CI datasets used for prediction.
- Enables a "predict-to-prescribe" clinical approach: use predicted outcomes to guide intervention decisions before surgery.
- Single model generalizes across languages and institutions — no country-specific or language-specific retraining required.
- Key input features derived from structural and functional brain connectivity patterns visible in pre-op MRI, not audiological data alone.
- Addresses longstanding clinical challenge: outcomes after CI implantation are highly variable and previously difficult to predict.
