## Key Facts

- Phonak Audéo Sphere Infinio launched August 2024 — first hearing aid in the world with a dedicated real-time AI processing chip (DEEPSONIC).
- DEEPSONIC chip runs a DNN trained on 22M+ sound samples, enabling scene-aware signal processing without relying on cloud compute.
- Spheric Speech Clarity 2.0 algorithm separates a target speaker from a surrounding sphere of competing sounds.
- October 2025 Ultra update: DNN retrained to be 30% more computationally efficient and 35% reduction in listening effort (per internal studies).
- Rated best-in-class in noise performance by HearAdvisor independent benchmarking.
- 56-hour battery life with charging case.
- Established the dedicated AI chip as a new category expectation in premium hearing aids, prompting competitive responses from Starkey (G3 NPU, 2025) and anticipated responses from Oticon and Signia.
