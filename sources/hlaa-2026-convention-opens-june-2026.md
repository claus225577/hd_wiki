# HLAA 2026 Convention Opens in Louisville

## Bibliographic
- **Organization:** Hearing Loss Association of America (HLAA)
- **Event:** 41st Annual HLAA Convention
- **Dates:** 10–12 June 2026
- **Location:** The Galt House Hotel, Louisville, Kentucky
- **URL:** https://hearingreview.com/inside-hearing/events/hlaa-2026-convention-to-convene-in-louisville

## What
HLAA's 41st annual convention is the largest US consumer-facing event for people with hearing loss, gathering audiologists, manufacturer representatives, accessibility advocates, and end-users. The 2026 edition is fully captioned with hearing loops in every meeting room.

The program centerpiece for the AI/data-science crowd is the Friday Research Symposium (12 Jun, 10am CT): **"The AI Revolution in Hearing Health: Smarter Tech, Greater Access."**

## Why It Matters
HLAA is the canonical consumer-advocacy voice in US hearing-loss policy. Their convention is where:
- Manufacturers preview features to a community that will publicly evaluate them within days.
- OTC, insurance, VA-related, and accessibility-act legislation is socialized to a base that calls Congress.
- Real-time captioning, hearing-loop, and Auracast deployments get tested at scale (every meeting room).

The Friday AI symposium is a useful temperature read on what the consumer side is actually willing to call AI — and what they consider hype vs. delivered value. Worth tracking what panelists do/don't choose to demo: DNN noise reduction is now table-stakes; on-demand AI clarity boost, conversation memory, and audiogram-driven personalization are the contested frontier.

## Context
- Coincides with the Veterans Hearing Aid Improvement Act push (HLAA is named as an endorsing organization on both House and Senate versions).
- Comes one week after BIHIMA Q1 2026 data showed UK private market +12.5% QoQ and rechargeable penetration at 93% — a useful comparator for US consumer adoption trends likely discussed at the convention.
