# Signia Insio Charge&Go CIC IX

## Summary
Signia (WS Audiology) made the **Insio Charge&Go CIC IX** available in the US market — a custom-fit, rechargeable, completely-in-canal (CIC) hearing aid running on the Signia IX platform with directional-microphone technology.

## Why It Matters
- Custom-fit + rechargeable + CIC is the single hardest physical envelope in hearing instruments. Achieving directional mics in CIC form factor is non-trivial because the acoustic baseline between mics is millimeters.
- Direct competitive answer to Phonak's Virto R Infinio (also custom + rechargeable in-the-ear, also 2026 generation, also award-winning industrial design).
- Reinforces the post-2024 industry shift: custom devices catch up to RIC on connectivity and rechargeability rather than being a "less feature-rich" subcategory.

## Open Questions
- Battery life under sustained streaming.
- Whether the IX-platform DNN features (Augmented Focus, Own Voice Processing) ship feature-complete on the CIC SoC, or are subset-only.
