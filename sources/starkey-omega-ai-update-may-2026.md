# Starkey Omega AI — Connectivity & Convenience Update (May 2026)

## Key Extractions

- **What:** Starkey blog post documenting the consolidated set of Omega AI platform updates rolled into the spring 2026 firmware wave.
- **Connectivity features added:**
  - **Auracast Assistant** (LE Audio broadcast reception)
  - **Push-to-Talk** (dedicated voice-capture / communication mode)
  - **Google Fast Pair** (one-tap Android pairing)
  - **StarLink Edge LE Adapter** — new accessory bridging non-LE source devices (legacy phones, TVs, conferencing systems) into the LE Audio stream the Omega AI aids receive.
- **DNN 360 performance claims:**
  - **+28% speech understanding** in noise.
  - **+8 dB SNR improvement.**
  - Marketed under the Omega AI "DNN 360" brand for the G3 Gen AI Neuro Processor's deep neural-network pipeline.
- **Awards stack:**
  - **2026 BIG Innovation Award** (Business Intelligence Group — distinct from the BIG AI Excellence Awards reported earlier in 2026).
  - **Edge AI + Vision Alliance award** — first hearing-aid recognition from the edge-AI computer-vision industry consortium.

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- **DNN 360 numbers as benchmark.** +28% speech / +8 dB SNR are the headline claims Starkey is now using to compete against Phonak's Sphere DEEPSONIC numbers and Oticon's Intent claims. The peer-reviewable version of these numbers does not yet exist; they sit alongside Phonak's clinical-trial publications (Apr 2026) on the marketing-vs-evidence axis.
- **StarLink Edge LE Adapter.** The first OEM-branded LE Audio bridge dongle from a top-5 hearing-aid manufacturer. Solves the source-side problem — wearers have LE-Audio-capable hearing aids but their TV / conference room / partner's phone does not. Structurally similar to Trihear ClearTV (June 2026) but vendor-locked to Starkey rather than cross-OEM.
- **Edge AI + Vision Alliance award.** Notable cross-industry signal — the same consortium that recognizes Qualcomm / Intel / Nvidia edge-AI vision silicon now recognizes a hearing-aid platform. Suggests hearing-aid AI is being read as a legitimate edge-AI category by the broader silicon community, not only by audiology trade press. Reinforces Achin Bhowmik's positioning (Intel perceptual-computing → Starkey CTO → AIMBE fellowship).
- **Three-OEM Auracast convergence (with Jan 2026 + May 2026 Govt Services rollouts):** Auracast Assistant is now generally available across commercial, government services, and the May update wave — Starkey's full installed base is broadcast-capable.
