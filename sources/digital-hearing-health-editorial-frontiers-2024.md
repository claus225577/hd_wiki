# Trends in Digital Hearing Health and Computational Audiology — Editorial

## Summary

Editorial in Frontiers in Neuroscience surveying the current state and emerging trends in digital hearing health and computational audiology. Serves as a topical overview of the Research Topic collection.

## Key Themes

- Integration of computational methods into routine audiological practice
- Digital health tools for hearing screening, monitoring, and rehabilitation
- The growing role of ML/AI in audiological assessment and intervention
- Challenges in clinical adoption of computational approaches

## Significance

Provides an authoritative framing of the computational audiology field from the Frontiers in Neuroscience perspective. Useful as a reference for understanding the current research landscape and identifying gaps.
