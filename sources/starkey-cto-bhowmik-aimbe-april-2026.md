# Starkey CTO Achin Bhowmik — AIMBE Induction

## Key Extraction

- **Person:** Achin Bhowmik, Chief Technology Officer & EVP of Engineering, Starkey
- **Recognition:** Inducted into the American Institute for Medical and Biological Engineering (AIMBE) College of Fellows
- **Date:** April 16, 2026
- **Reason:** Recognition of AI-driven hearing innovation leadership
- **Significance:**
  - AIMBE fellowship is a prestigious recognition in medical/biomedical engineering; induction signals that AI-driven hearing aid technology is being recognized by the broader biomedical engineering establishment
  - Bhowmik's background (previously VP at Intel, perceptual computing) reflects the cross-pollination of consumer tech AI talent into hearing aid industry
  - Validates Starkey's health-tech positioning — their CTO is recognized for medical/biological engineering, not just consumer electronics
  - Starkey is the only major hearing aid manufacturer with a CTO from the consumer AI/computing world (Intel perceptual computing → hearing aids)
