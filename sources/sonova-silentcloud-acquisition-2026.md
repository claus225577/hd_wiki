# Sonova Acquires SilentCloud

## Summary
Sonova announced the acquisition of **SilentCloud**, a digital tinnitus-therapy platform first launched in 2023. The acquisition brings SilentCloud's development in-house and folds digital tinnitus therapy into Sonova's hearing-care ecosystem.

## Background
- SilentCloud is a tinnitus management app first launched in 2023 (verify dates — *not* a 2026 product launch).
- Tinnitus management has historically been ancillary to Sonova's core hearing-aid business; this signals a strategic move to make digital therapeutics a first-class part of the hearing-care funnel.

## Strategic Read
- Aligns with Sonova's stated CHF 6B-by-2030 sales-target framework, which leans heavily on broadening from hardware-only into integrated services.
- Tinnitus is high-prevalence (10–15% of adults) and chronically under-served by pure-audio hardware; an in-house digital therapy gives clinics a non-hardware monetization lane.
- Competitive context: Demant/Oticon has had similar telecare/app moves; WS Audiology recently launched its **Sound Preference** program (April 20, 2026).

## Caveats
- SilentCloud's clinical evidence base predates the acquisition; integration may require updated regulatory filings depending on geography (EU MDR / FDA).
- Past wiki memory flagged a date-confusion risk on SilentCloud — its product *first* launched 2023, with the Sonova *acquisition* being the 2026 news.
