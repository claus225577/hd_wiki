# Ceretone OTC Hearing Aids UK Launch

## Key Extraction

- **Company:** Ceretone — OTC/DTC hearing aid brand
- **Product:** Ready-to-wear hearing aids launching in the UK
- **Price:** £129.99 — extremely low price point
- **Date:** April 15, 2026
- **Significance:**
  - At £129.99 (~$165 USD), this is one of the cheapest OTC hearing aids to reach a major market
  - Undercuts Apple AirPods Pro 2 ($249) as hearing aid by ~35%
  - UK does not have an FDA-equivalent OTC hearing aid category; products sold as "personal sound amplification" or under UK medical device regulations
  - Demonstrates that the OTC hearing aid price floor is still dropping
  - "Ready to wear" implies no app-based fitting, preset amplification profiles — simplest category of OTC
  - International expansion of affordable OTC beyond the US market
  - Competitive pressure on both premium OTC brands (Jabra, Eargo) and NHS hearing aid provision
  - Raises quality/safety questions at this price point — what DNN/DSP capability exists at £129.99?
