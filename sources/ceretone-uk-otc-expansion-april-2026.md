# Ceretone Brings Affordable Ready-to-Wear Hearing Aids to UK

## Key Facts
- Following a UK law change allowing non-prescription hearing aids
- Ceretone entered British market with affordable ready-to-wear options
- Priced at a fraction of traditional prescription hearing aid costs
- No audiologist appointment required

## Key Extraction
- UK regulatory shift mirrors US OTC hearing aid pathway (FDA 2022)
- Global regulatory convergence toward consumer-accessible hearing aids
- International expansion of OTC model validates the category's viability

## Relevance to Hearing + AI
- Global OTC expansion increases total addressable market for AI-powered hearing devices
- Regulatory harmonization across US, UK, EU creates opportunities for AI features that work across markets
- Self-fitting algorithms become more critical as audiologist-free purchase paths expand
