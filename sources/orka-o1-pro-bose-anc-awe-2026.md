# Orka O1 Pro with Bose ANC

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Orka O1 Pro — world's first RIC (receiver-in-canal) hearing aid with active noise cancellation
- **ANC Partner:** Bose — integrates Bose QuietControl ANC technology
- **Speech Enhancement:** Bose SpeechClarity feature for speech-in-noise improvement
- **Weight:** 3.5 grams — lightweight for a feature-dense RIC
- **Battery Life:** 35+ hours — exceptional for a device with ANC running
- **Debut:** AWE (Augmented World Expo) 2026
- **Significance:**
  - First hearing aid to combine prescription-grade amplification with consumer-grade ANC
  - Bose partnership bridges the gap between consumer audio and medical hearing tech
  - ANC in hearing aids addresses a different need than noise reduction: ANC attenuates environmental noise physically, while DNN noise reduction separates speech from noise computationally
  - 3.5g weight and 35hr battery suggest significant engineering to offset ANC power overhead
  - Debuting at AWE (not AAA) signals Orka's positioning as a tech/consumer brand vs. traditional audiology

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- ANC + DNN speech enhancement is a dual-layer noise management approach not previously available in hearing aids
- Bose QuietControl brings consumer audio technology expertise into medical hearing devices
- Potential paradigm: ANC handles broadband environmental noise, freeing DNN capacity for finer speech-in-noise enhancement
- Competitive pressure on Big 6 to integrate ANC or partner with consumer audio companies
- Weight and battery life metrics set new expectations for feature-rich hearing aids
