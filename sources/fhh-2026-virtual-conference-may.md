# Future of Hearing Healthcare 2026 — Sixth Annual Virtual Conference

## Key Facts

- **Conference:** Future of Hearing Healthcare (FHH) 2026
- **Edition:** 6th annual
- **Format:** Virtual / online (Accelevents platform)
- **Dates:** Three Wednesdays — May 13, 20, and 27, 2026
- **Sessions:** 23+ expert-led sessions across the three days
- **Format note:** Each conference day closes with a panel discussion that synthesizes the day's themes
- **Registration:** https://www.accelevents.com/e/fhh-2026
- **Audience:** Hearing care clinicians, researchers, technologists, industry leaders
- **CEUs:** Available; on-demand sessions accessible after live event

## Program Themes (per organizer's announcement)

- Growing role of artificial intelligence in hearing care
- Hearing aid technology advancements
- Wireless connectivity (Auracast / Bluetooth LE Audio rollout context)
- Patient engagement and outcomes models
- Clinical best practices
- Emerging technologies and evolving care delivery models

## Why This Is Notable

FHH is the most consistently AI-and-tech-forward of the audiology conference circuit — its virtual format and free / low-cost access make it an unusually broad-tent venue compared to AAA, ASHA, or the regional academies. The 2026 lineup explicitly foregrounds AI in hearing care as a thematic pillar (not just a session track), reflecting how rapidly the conversation has shifted in the last 12 months.

It also slots between two major in-person events — CI2026 Chicago (May 6–9) and the World Congress of Audiology in Seoul (May 24–27) — making the May 13/20/27 cadence a useful synthesis venue for what was just presented in Chicago and is about to be presented in Seoul.
