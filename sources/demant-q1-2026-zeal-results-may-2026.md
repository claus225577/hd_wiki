# Demant Q1 2026 Results — Oticon Zeal Drives the Quarter

## Headline Numbers

- **Total revenue: +16%** (vs. consensus +13.5%); breakdown: **+6% organic** + **+10% from acquisitions**.
- **Hearing Aids organic growth (external customers): +9%**, driven by the global rollout of **Oticon Zeal** (rechargeable ITE, AAA 2026 debut).
- **Gross margin expanded** vs. Q1 2025 on higher ASPs in Hearing Aids and positive business mix.
- **Share reaction: +~11% on the print** (some intra-day prints quoted ~15-16%).

## Market Read (per Demant)

- **Global hearing-aid market Q1 2026: +4% in value** — at the upper end of Demant's assumed 2–4% range for 2026.
- Decomposition: **+3% unit growth + +1% positive ASP contribution**.
- Demant's 9% organic HA growth vs. 3% global units → **clear share gain** in Q1.

## 2026 Outlook

- **Maintained** at 3–6% organic growth and **DKK 4.1–4.5B EBIT before special items**.
- Management commentary: strong reception of Oticon Zeal makes results at the **lower end of those ranges less likely** — implicit upward skew within the band.

## Cost Programme

- On track for **DKK 250M (~$39M)** savings in 2026.
- **DKK 500M (~$78M)** total savings expected by 2028 (full effect).

## Why It Matters

1. **Second confirmation of market top-of-band growth** within two weeks — Sonova's FY 2025/26 print already signalled the same. The 2026 hearing-aid value cycle is running hot relative to the 5-year ASP-driven baseline. See [[hearing-aid-market-dynamics]] and [[hearing-aid-market-outlook]].
2. **Zeal's launch trajectory is now the single most important BTE/RIC product story of the year** — Oticon's first rechargeable ITE on the Sirius platform with full DNN 2.0 parity. Share-gain evidence here matters because ITE is the form factor most exposed to OTC encroachment and where Apple AirPods Pro 2/3 hearing features compete most directly.
3. **VA contract addition** for Zeal (May 2026) likely contributes — VA is the largest single US payer for hearing aids. See [[../sources/oticon-va-portfolio-zeal-intent-verit-may-2026]].
