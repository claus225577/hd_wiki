# Auracast Venue Deployments — UK Theatres + Sydney Opera House + CCI UAL (2026)

## Key Extractions

HearingTracker's running Auracast venue tracker documents a new wave of high-profile cultural / academic venue installations in 2026:

- **National Theatre — Dorfman Theatre (London):** Auracast trialed during the run of *Man and Boy* — first National Theatre auditorium to test Auracast assistive listening.
- **Bridge Theatre (London):** Auracast deployment for general audience access.
- **Sydney Opera House (Australia):** Auracast installation — the first major Southern Hemisphere cultural-flagship venue to ship Auracast publicly. Pairs with the GN/Ampetronic Seoul jazz-club deployment (May 26, 2026) as the second Asia-Pacific venue install.
- **CCI — Creative Computing Institute, University of the Arts London (UAL):** Higher-education / studio-environment Auracast deployment. Notable as the first academic-creative-space installation rather than a performance venue.

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- **Beyond pilot venues.** The earlier UK theatre deployments (Everyman Cheltenham, Contact Manchester, April 2026) were regional pilots. The Dorfman + Bridge + Sydney Opera House cluster is the first set of *flagship* cultural-venue installs — venues whose audience size and brand reach materially shift Auracast's social proof beyond the accessibility-advocacy community.
- **Geographic broadening.** Three continents now have permanent or trial Auracast venue installs in 2026 (Europe — UK theatres + NHS pilots; Asia — Seoul jazz club; Oceania — Sydney Opera House). The hardware-side problem (Auri / Ampetronic transmitters) is solved; the bottleneck is venue procurement cycles.
- **Academic-creative venue category.** CCI at UAL is the first documented Auracast deployment in a creative-academic environment (studio, lecture, workshop). Expands the deployment surface beyond performance / transit / clinical settings.
- **Pairs with the Sydney + Big Ocean K-pop / GN-Ampetronic Seoul thread.** Both 2026 Q2 milestones use the Auri Auracast hardware family — same vendor, different continents.
- **Cumulative tally.** With this wave, mid-2026 Auracast venue installs span: regional UK theatres, flagship UK theatres, NHS clinics, European airport pilots, transit-system pilots, Korean cultural venues, Australian flagship cultural venue, UK academic-creative space. The "chicken-and-egg" framing (devices first, venues later) is decisively over on the device side and entering rapid catch-up on the venue side.
