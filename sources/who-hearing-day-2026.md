# WHO World Hearing Day 2026 — Hearing Care for All Children

## Key Extractions
- WHO World Hearing Day 2026 theme: "From communities to classrooms: hearing care for all children"
- 90 million children and adolescents aged 5-19 affected by hearing loss globally
- More than 60% of childhood hearing loss is preventable
- Campaign focus: integrating hearing care into existing school health programs
- Small Grants Program offering $800 USD to community organizations for local hearing initiatives
- Cross-sectoral partnership emphasis: connecting hearing care with child health, education, and eye health programs
- Goal of embedding hearing screening into routine pediatric and school health touchpoints
- Addresses access gap where most childhood hearing loss goes undetected and untreated in low- and middle-income countries

## Relevance
The school integration angle is strategically important — it normalizes hearing screening as part of routine child health rather than specialist referral. The 60% preventability figure underscores that this is as much a public health systems problem as a technology problem.
