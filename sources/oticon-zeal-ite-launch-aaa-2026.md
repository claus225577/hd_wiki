# Oticon Zeal ITE Launch at AAA 2026

## Key Facts

- **Oticon Zeal** is a new rechargeable in-the-ear (ITE) hearing aid debuting at the AAA 2026 conference on April 22, 2026
- Represents Demant/Oticon's entry into the modern rechargeable ITE segment
- Features **streaming** and **connectivity** capabilities — previously difficult to achieve in ITE form factors due to antenna and battery size constraints
- Debuting at the American Academy of Audiology (AAA) annual conference, the largest US audiology trade event
- Complements Oticon's existing BTE/RIC lineup (Intent, Verit, Play SI) with an ITE option

## Significance

- ITE hearing aids are preferred by some users for cosmetic reasons (less visible than BTE/RIC styles)
- Adding rechargeable + streaming to ITE addresses a long-standing gap — most advanced features have been RIC/BTE-first
- Demonstrates Demant's breadth strategy: covering all form factors with modern AI and connectivity features
- AAA 2026 launch timing puts Zeal alongside Verit (premium BTE) and Play SI (pediatric) in a comprehensive spring 2026 product blitz

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- ITE form factor imposes tighter power and space constraints than BTE/RIC — on-device ML inference must be even more efficient
- Streaming in ITE requires efficient Bluetooth LE implementation; likely Auracast-compatible given Oticon's recent Auracast push (Verit, Play SI)
- Rechargeable ITE with connectivity represents the engineering convergence of battery tech, antenna miniaturization, and low-power AI processing
