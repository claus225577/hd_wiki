# Deep Learning Framework for Understanding Cochlear Implants

## Summary

Combines 3D-printed biomimetic cochleae with machine learning in a co-modelling framework. Refines interpretation of electric field imaging (EFI) profiles to predict patient-specific cochlear anatomy and tissue resistivity. Published 2025 in PMC.

## Key Contributions

- 3D-printed biomimetic cochleae as physical models for validation
- ML interpretation of electric field imaging profiles
- Predicts patient-specific anatomy and tissue resistivity
- Personalized clinical insights for CI fitting
- Co-modelling framework bridging physical and computational models

## Significance

Current CI fitting is largely trial-and-error. This framework enables predicting how electrical stimulation spreads through an individual patient's cochlea, potentially enabling more precise electrode programming and better speech perception outcomes.
