# Cearvol NeuroFlow AI 2.0 Platform

## Summary
At **CES 2026**, OTC hearing-aid maker Cearvol unveiled its **NeuroFlow AI 2.0** intelligent hearing system — a deep-neural-network-based audio processing platform.

## Reported Performance
- **~24% improvement** in speech enhancement vs. the previous generation.
- **Up to 20 dB** of noise reduction.
- (Vendor-reported numbers — not independent benchmarks.)

## Why It Matters
- A useful **public benchmark** for OTC AI claims: independent labs and reviewers can use these numbers as a yardstick for competing OTC platforms.
- Indicates that OTC manufacturers are now publishing DNN performance metrics in the same vocabulary (SI-SDR-style improvements, dB NR) that prescription brands and academic papers use.
- The OTC tier is closing the gap on speech-enhancement performance with the prescription tier — at least on vendor-reported benchmarks.

## Caveats
- Vendor-reported metrics; no public test-set details, no independent replication.
- "24% improvement" is unspecified — improvement of what metric (PESQ, STOI, SI-SDR, subjective rating)?
- Real-world performance gap to top-tier prescription DNNs (Phonak Sphere, Oticon Intent) remains untested.
