# Kimi K2.6 — Open-Source Agentic Model

## Key Facts
- Moonshot AI open-sourced **Kimi K2.6**
- Supports **300 sub-agent swarm coordination**
- Capable of **4,000 coordinated steps** in a single task
- Achieves **80.2% on SWE-Bench** (software engineering benchmark)
- Represents frontier of agentic AI capabilities
