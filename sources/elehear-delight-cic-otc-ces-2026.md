# Elehear Delight CIC OTC Hearing Aid Unveiled at CES 2026

## Summary
At CES 2026 (13 Jan 2026, Las Vegas), Elehear introduced **Delight**, the company's first completely-in-canal (CIC) OTC hearing aid. The launch is notable because the OTC category has been overwhelmingly BTE/RIC since the 2022 FDA rule — a CIC OTC at sub-$300 changes the discretion/cosmesis axis of the OTC market.

## Key Specs
- **Form factor:** CIC with ergonomic ear-wing retention structure
- **Amplification:** Up to 45 dB (mild-to-moderate range)
- **Noise reduction:** Up to 24 dB attenuation, AI-driven
- **AI engine:** "VocClear" — real-time sound environment analysis
- **Feedback management:** Smart suppression
- **Translation:** AI-powered real-time translation across 20 languages
- **Tinnitus:** "Serene Sound Relief" white-noise mode
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth 5.3
- **Ingress protection:** IP67
- **Regulatory:** FDA-registered OTC

## Commercial Terms
- **Early-bird pre-order:** $299 (limited)
- **Trial:** 60-day extended
- **Managing Director:** David Hogan

## Why It Matters
1. **CIC OTC opens new geometry.** Visibility/stigma is one of the most-cited barriers to OTC uptake; CIC removes the visible-RIC tell.
2. **On-device translation as wedge feature.** Translation is a high-engagement use case that exists outside the medical-hearing positioning — a route to attract non-traditional users (frequent travelers, multilingual households) into the funnel.
3. **Sub-$300 + 60-day trial** keeps pace with the aggressive pricing-and-returns regime that characterizes the post-AirPods-Hearing-Aid OTC market.

## Open Questions
