# Oticon Play SI Launch

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Oticon Play SI — pediatric hearing aid
- **AI:** Second-generation AI sound processing (same gen as Verit)
- **Sensors:** 4D user-intent sensors (head motion, body motion, conversation activity, acoustic environment) — previously exclusive to adult Oticon Intent, now brought to pediatric line
- **Connectivity:**
  - Bluetooth LE Audio with Auracast
  - Made for iPhone (MFi)
  - Android Fast Pair
- **Launch date:** April 16, 2026
- **Significance:**
  - First pediatric hearing aid with 4D sensor technology
  - First pediatric hearing aid with LE Audio/Auracast — critical for classroom accessibility (replaces FM/Roger systems over time)
  - Cross-platform connectivity (iOS + Android) addresses the reality that children's parents use diverse smartphone ecosystems
  - Pediatric AI hearing aids were previously limited; this brings flagship-level AI to children
- **Noise suppression:** 12dB noise suppression
- **Bandwidth:** 80Hz–10kHz
- **Durability:** IP68 rated (dust/water)
- **Styles:** miniRITE R and miniBTE R
- **Accessories:** EduMic compatible — important for classroom use alongside Auracast
