# Signia Integrated Xperience (IX) Platform

## Key Extractions
- Integrated Xperience (IX) is Signia's flagship AI platform
- Multi-stream technology processes own voice and surrounding sounds as separate streams simultaneously
- RealTime Conversation Enhancement adapts to rapid changes in conversation dynamics
- Claims 22% better speech understanding compared to AI co-processor competitors
- Signia Assistant uses a cloud-based DNN for remote fine-tuning and user adaptation
- Platform expanded to ITC, ITE, and BTE form factors in 2025

## Relevance
Signia's multi-stream separation of own-voice vs. environment is a distinct architectural choice compared to single unified DNN models. The 22% speech intelligibility claim against co-processor competitors is a key benchmark to validate independently. Cloud-based DNN assistant model raises interesting questions about on-device vs. cloud inference trade-offs in hearing aid UX.
