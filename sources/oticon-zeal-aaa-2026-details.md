# Oticon Zeal at AAA 2026 — ITE with Encapsulation Tech

**Announced:** April 10, 2026
**Showcased:** AAA 2026 Conference, April 22-24, San Antonio, TX

## Key Details

- **Oticon Zeal:** New rechargeable in-the-ear (ITE) hearing aid
- **Encapsulation technology:** New manufacturing approach for ITE form factor — likely improves durability and moisture resistance for the custom-shaped shell
- **Rechargeable:** Brings lithium-ion rechargeability to ITE form factor (historically challenging due to battery size constraints in custom shells)
- **Auracast demos:** Live Auracast broadcast demonstrations at the AAA booth — showing Bluetooth LE Audio streaming to the ITE form factor
- **Play SI pediatric solutions:** Also showcased alongside Zeal at AAA, with clinical data and demos

## AAA 2026 Context

- AAA (American Academy of Audiology) 2026 conference: April 22-24, San Antonio, TX
- Major annual industry event for product launches and clinical presentations
- Demant/Oticon showing broadest spring 2026 product lineup: Verit (adult), Play SI (pediatric), Zeal (ITE) — three products spanning all major form factors and demographics

## Significance

- **ITE form factor modernization:** ITE hearing aids have historically lagged BTE/RIC in features (connectivity, rechargeability, AI processing) due to tighter space/power constraints
- **Encapsulation tech:** May represent a manufacturing innovation that enables new component integration in custom ITE shells
- **Auracast in ITE:** If confirmed, extends Bluetooth LE Audio to the ITE form factor — demonstrating that the full connectivity stack can fit in-ear
- Continues Demant's spring 2026 product blitz (Verit + Play SI + Zeal = broadest form factor coverage in the industry)
