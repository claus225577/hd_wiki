# Oticon Play SI — Hearing Review Coverage

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Oticon Play SI pediatric hearing aid family
- **AI:** 2nd-generation AI sound processing
- **Noise suppression:** 12 dB
- **Bandwidth:** 80 Hz - 10 kHz
- **Sensors:** 4D user-intent sensors (head motion, body motion, conversation activity, acoustic environment)
- **Connectivity:** BLE Audio (Auracast-ready), Made for iPhone (MFi), Android Fast Pair

### Clinical Validation (Boys Town National Research Hospital)
- **50% improved sentence recognition** in noise
- **77% reduced listening difficulty** for teenagers
- Study conducted at Boys Town — one of the most respected pediatric audiology research centers

### Additional Technical Details
- Auracast-ready for classroom broadcast audio
- Cross-platform connectivity (iOS + Android)

## Relevance to Hearing + AI

- **AI crossing trust threshold into pediatric audiology** — clinical validation from Boys Town (independent research center) builds evidence for AI hearing aids in children
- **Pediatric-specific validation gap:** Most DNN hearing aid evidence is from adult studies; this addresses a critical gap
- **Teen listening difficulty reduction (77%)** is particularly significant because teens face unique listening challenges (classroom noise, social environments) and are less likely to comply with hearing aid use if the device doesn't help enough
- **50% sentence recognition improvement** quantifies the benefit of 2nd-gen AI processing for children — stronger than typical adult improvements reported for DNN hearing aids
