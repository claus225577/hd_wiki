# Apple AirPods Pro Clinical-Grade Hearing Aid Feature

## Key Extractions
- AirPods Pro 2 and 3 support clinical-grade hearing aid functionality
- Built-in pure-tone audiometry test completes in approximately 5 minutes
- Classified as FDA over-the-counter (OTC) hearing aid
- Personalized dynamic sound adjustments based on audiometry results
- Available in 150+ countries
- Requires iOS 26 / iPadOS 26 or later

## Relevance
Apple's entry into the clinical hearing aid space via software update to existing hardware is the most significant distribution event in OTC hearing aid history. Reaching AirPods Pro's installed base of tens of millions of users dwarfs the traditional hearing aid market. The audiometry-to-fitting pipeline in a consumer device sets a new baseline for accessibility and will accelerate pressure on incumbent manufacturers on price and convenience.
