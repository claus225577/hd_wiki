# Phonak USA Pickleball Partnership

## Key Facts

- **Campaign name:** "Hear to Win"
- **Launch:** Spring 2026
- **Partner:** USA Pickleball (national governing body)
- **Focus:** Hearing health awareness through pickleball community
- **Duration:** Running throughout tournament season (spring-fall 2026)

## Extraction

Phonak's "Hear to Win" partnership with USA Pickleball targets one of the fastest-growing sports in the US — a sport whose demographic skews toward the 50+ age group that overlaps significantly with hearing loss prevalence. This is a strategic marketing move: rather than traditional medical/clinical messaging, Phonak is embedding hearing health awareness into an active-lifestyle context. The partnership signals Phonak/Sonova's shift toward consumer-brand marketing tactics alongside traditional clinical channels, consistent with the industry's response to OTC competition and consumer tech encroachment.
