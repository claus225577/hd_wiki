# AAA 2026 Convention — Full Program Details

## Key Facts

- **Theme:** "Amplify Your Value"
- **Dates:** April 22-25, 2026 (extended through April 25)
- **Location:** San Antonio, Texas
- **Keynote:** Dr. Hassan Tetteh — "The Art of Human Care" — blending AI with empathy in healthcare
- **Scale:** 200+ sessions, 130+ exhibitors, 16 tracks
- **New track:** "Amplify Your Value" track added for 2026
- **Key exhibitors:** Oticon (Zeal launch), Starkey (Omega AI showcase), Phonak, ReSound, Signia, Cochlear
- **Oticon Foundation donation:** $30K to AAA Foundation for student scholarships

## Extraction

The 2026 AAA Convention is the largest edition in recent years with 200+ sessions across 16 tracks and 130+ exhibitors. Dr. Hassan Tetteh's keynote on "The Art of Human Care" explicitly addresses the AI-empathy balance in healthcare, reflecting the industry's awareness that AI adoption must be paired with human-centered care. The new "Amplify Your Value" track underscores the profession's strategic response to OTC disruption and consumer tech encroachment. Oticon's $30K scholarship donation signals manufacturer investment in the audiology pipeline amid workforce shortage concerns.
