# AI-Enabled Innovations in Cochlear Implant Technology

## Key Extractions
- Review covers AI-enabled innovations across the full CI technology stack
- Personalized rehabilitation strategies powered by AI, adapting to individual user progress and audiological profiles
- Advanced algorithms enhancing device adaptability to changing listening environments
- Deep learning for speech recognition achieving performance matching the best human CI users
- End-to-end audio-visual learning for CI sound coding in noise — integrating lip-reading cues with audio processing
- AI enabling more granular mapping and fitting of CI devices beyond traditional manual audiologist programming
- Emerging focus on continuous learning systems that improve over device lifetime

## Relevance
Comprehensive review of where AI intersects with CI at every level — from signal coding to rehabilitation. Audio-visual end-to-end learning is a particularly significant direction for noisy environment performance.
