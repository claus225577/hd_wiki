# MarkeTrak 2025 — Hearing Aid Adoption and OTC Survey

## Key Extractions
- Hearing aid adoption rate rose from 30.2% (2015) to 39.1% (2025) among those with hearing difficulty
- When cochlear implants and other implants are included, adoption reaches 43%
- OTC hearing aid adoption stands at 5.7% of the hearing aid market since FDA OTC rule
- 20.9% of survey respondents use earbuds or consumer earphones with hearing enhancement features
- Self-reported hearing difficulty affects 9.1% of the US population
- Survey methodology: 16,148 households surveyed

## Relevance
MarkeTrak is the primary longitudinal data source for US hearing aid market trends. The 9-point adoption increase over a decade is notable but leaves the majority of people with hearing difficulty still untreated. The 20.9% earbud-with-hearing-features figure is the most significant new data point — it suggests consumer devices are already functioning as a parallel hearing assistance channel well ahead of formal OTC regulation impact. This directly informs how to frame Apple AirPods and similar entries.
