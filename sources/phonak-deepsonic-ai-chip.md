# Phonak DEEPSONIC AI Chip

- 4.5 million neural connections in the on-device DNN
- Trained on 22 million sound scenes
- Dual-chip architecture separates AI processing from audio I/O
- Spheric Speech Clarity 2.0 is the flagship DNN-powered feature
- Ultra firmware update released October 2025, trained on 18x more real-world data than predecessor
- Won the 2026 AI Excellence Award
