# Amazon Prime Day 2026 — OTC Hearing Aid Pricing

## Event
Amazon Prime Day ran 23-26 Jun 2026 with material discounting across the OTC hearing-aid category. HearingTracker tracked the deals as a featured editorial.

## Notable Price Points
- **Cearvol Wave:** $377 (saving $120 vs. regular $497) — also a deeper $749.95 promo on a different SKU.
- **Cearvol Wave Lite:** $299 (saving $90 vs. regular $389).
- Broad participation across other OTC brands (Eargo, Lexie, JLab, Audien, etc., per HearingTracker editorial).

## Why It Matters for Hearing AI
- **Retail channel maturation.** Prime Day is now a meaningful share-shifting window for budget OTC brands — the channel is no longer purely audiology-led even seasonally.
- **Price-floor signal for the lower end of the AI-enabled OTC market.** At sub-$300 the on-board ML feature set has to be hardware-efficient (sub-mW, sub-MB) to fit the BOM — directly relevant to the `wiki/concepts/tinyml-edge-ai.md` story.
- **Competitive pressure on premium OTC.** Apple AirPods Pro 2 at $249 (Hearing Aid feature included) anchors the very bottom; the Prime Day pricing on Cearvol Wave Lite ($299) shows the squeeze.
- **Pulls reimbursement attention** — when retail OTC drops below the typical sales-tax-inclusive private-pay threshold for a single device, payers re-evaluate whether to bundle vs. reimburse separately.
