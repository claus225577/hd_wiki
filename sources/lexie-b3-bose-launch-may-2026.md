# Lexie B3 OTC Hearing Aids Powered by Bose (May 2026)

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Lexie B3 — receiver-in-canal (RIC), rechargeable, self-fitting OTC hearing aid for adults with perceived mild-to-moderate hearing loss.
- **Price:** $999/pair on Lexie's website. Mid-tier OTC positioning — above ultra-affordable (Ceretone ~$165, AirPods Pro 3 $200) and below prescription.
- **Battery:** 32 hrs per charge from the aid; carrying case adds 96 hrs ⇒ ~5 days total runtime. Lexie claims 75% longer than the B2 Plus.
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth 5.3 with both Bluetooth Classic and BLE; iOS + Android streaming for music, video, hands-free calls. BLE support is the foundation for Auracast-readiness.
- **New feature:** "Automatic Sound Focus" — directional system that uses the dual mics to steer toward the dominant speech direction and reduce background noise.
- **Manufacturer linkage:** Lexie is backed by WS Audiology; Bose contributes the audio-tuning IP. This is WSA's continuing OTC play after the April 2026 Sony exit.

## Why This Matters for Hearing AI

- **Strategic re-entry for WSA in OTC.** With Sony OTC discontinued (April 2026), WSA needs a credible OTC vehicle. Lexie + Bose at $999 is the answer — same price band Sony failed in, but with a stronger app + brand-trust story and 5-day battery.
- **Mid-priced OTC stress test.** Sony's exit suggested the $700–$1,300 OTC band is unviable. Lexie B3 is the next data point. If it succeeds, the failure was Sony-specific (poor follow-up care, weak app); if it fails, the "middle is dead" thesis hardens.
- **BLE 5.3 baseline is now standard.** Even mid-priced OTC ships with BLE — Auracast-readiness is becoming table stakes, not a premium feature. Mirrors the Signia/Starkey/Oticon "Auracast-ready" status earlier in 2025/26.
- **Automatic Sound Focus is a directional-DNN positioning play.** Bose's spatial-audio expertise feeding into a HA directional system is one of the more interesting consumer-audio-to-medical-hearing IP transfers we've seen — comparable to Orka × Bose ANC partnership.

## Open Questions
