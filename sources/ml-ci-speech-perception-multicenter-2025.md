# ML for CI Speech Perception Prediction — Largest Study to Date

## Summary

Analyzes one of the largest CI patient datasets to date — 4,046 patients across 32 US institutions — to assess feasibility of ML for predicting post-implant speech perception performance. Published in Ear and Hearing, September 2025.

## Key Contributions

- 4,046 patients — unprecedented scale for CI outcome prediction
- 32 institutions — multicenter validation
- Assesses ML feasibility for predicting who will do well with a CI vs. poorly
- Potential for pre-operative counseling and candidacy decisions

## Significance

CI outcomes vary enormously across patients (some achieve near-normal speech perception, others get minimal benefit). Predicting outcomes pre-operatively would transform candidacy decisions and patient counseling. The scale of this dataset (4,046 patients) provides statistical power that smaller single-center studies lacked.
