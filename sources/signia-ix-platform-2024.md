## Key Facts

- Signia Integrated Xperience (IX) platform launched 2024; expanded August 2025 with Insio IX (ITE form factors) and Motion Charge&Go IX (BTE).
- RealTime Conversation Enhancement: simultaneous multi-stream processing to track both own-voice and conversation partner voices independently.
- Multi-stream architecture enables real-time separation of own-voice, primary speaker, and background noise as distinct audio objects.
- 8.1 dB SNR benefit unaided baseline; 3.2 dB advantage over competing hearing aids in independent testing conditions.
- Cloud-based live DNN updates delivered via Signia Assistant app, allowing algorithm improvements without hardware replacement.
- 85% increase in reported time willing to spend in noisy group conversation settings — a patient-centered outcome measure.
- WS Audiology (parent company, also owner of Widex) applying IX technology as platform foundation for multiple brands.
