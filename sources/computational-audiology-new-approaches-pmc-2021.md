# Computational Audiology: Foundational Review

## Summary

Foundational review paper defining the field of computational audiology and its potential to advance hearing healthcare in the digital age. Published in PMC/Ear and Hearing, 2021. Cited extensively by VCCA conference community.

## Key Contributions

- Defines "computational audiology" as a field
- Maps the landscape: automated audiometry, ML for diagnosis, digital phenotyping, remote assessment
- Articulates the vision for data-driven, AI-augmented audiology
- Serves as the conceptual foundation for the VCCA conference series

## Significance

This is the foundational/definitional paper for the computational audiology field. Essential reference for understanding the intellectual framework that VCCA and related research builds upon.
