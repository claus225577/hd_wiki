# Oticon Verit Launch

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Oticon Verit — new premium adult hearing aid from Demant/Oticon
- **AI:** Second-generation AI sound processing (successor to DNN 2.0 in Oticon Intent)
- **Connectivity:**
