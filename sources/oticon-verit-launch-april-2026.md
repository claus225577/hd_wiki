# Oticon Verit Launch

## Key Extraction

- **Product:** Oticon Verit — new premium adult hearing aid from Demant/Oticon
- **AI:** Second-generation AI sound processing (successor to DNN 2.0 in Oticon Intent)
- **Connectivity:**
  - Bluetooth LE Audio with Auracast broadcast support
  - Google Fast Pair for seamless Android pairing
- **Positioning:** Premium tier, aimed at adults with hearing loss
- **Launch date:** April 15, 2026
- **Significance:** First Oticon product with both Auracast and Google Fast Pair; signals Demant's commitment to LE Audio ecosystem and cross-platform compatibility (iOS + Android)
- **Platform:** Sirius platform (confirmed — same SoC family as Oticon Intent)
- **Noise suppression:** 12dB noise suppression — 2dB better than Oticon Real, 3dB better than Opn S
- **Battery:** Zinc-air disposable battery styles (differentiator vs rechargeable-only Intent)
- **SuddenSound Stabilizer:** Feature for managing sudden loud sounds
- **MoreSound Intelligence 3.0:** Latest generation of Oticon's AI scene classification and sound processing system
- **AI generation:** Second-gen AI sound processing on Sirius platform — evolution beyond DNN 2.0 in Oticon Intent (2024)
- **BLE Audio:** Full Bluetooth LE Audio support for streaming and Auracast
