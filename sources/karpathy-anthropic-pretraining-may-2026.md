# Andrej Karpathy Joins Anthropic Pre-Training Team

## Key Facts

- **Announced:** May 19, 2026, via Karpathy on X
- **Role:** Joining Anthropic to work on **pre-training**, reporting to **Nick Joseph** (head of pretraining)
- **Mandate per Anthropic spokesperson (TechCrunch):** start a team focused on **using Claude to accelerate pre-training research**

## Karpathy's Direct Quote

> "I've joined Anthropic. I think the next few years at the frontier of LLMs will be especially formative. I am very excited to join the team here and get back to R&D."

## Background

- Karpathy is an OpenAI co-founder, former Tesla AI Director (Autopilot)
- Author of widely-cited educational materials on neural networks, transformers, LLMs
- Known for the **Software 3.0** framing (Sequoia AI Ascent 2026), **Autoresearch** project, idea-files, LLM Wiki concept
- Has been operating independently in 2025–2026 (educational content, Sequoia talk, Autoresearch experiments)

## Industry Framing

- For ~18 months the visible action in AI has been in **post-training** (RLHF, agents, tool use, alignment, scaffolding)
- A leading practitioner choosing **pre-training** as his next chapter is read as a directional signal that the next leg of capability growth is believed to live in the base model
- Anthropic explicitly frames the hire as **AI-assisted research** (using Claude to accelerate pre-training) — recursive AI-improves-AI operationalised in the most expensive R&D loop
- Pre-training is the most compute-intensive phase of building a frontier model

## Relevance to Hearing-Care AI

- Hearing-care AI industry has implicitly bet on the **fine-tune-on-top-of-someone-else's-foundation-model** trajectory (adapters on Whisper, LoRA on Voxtral, distillation from frontier LLMs)
- OEMs (Sonova, Demant, GN, WSA, Starkey) uniquely own multi-decade longitudinal corpora: audiograms, in-situ telemetry, fitting outcomes, real-world acoustic context, paired at the patient level
- That corpus is too small to train a general foundation model from scratch and too important to leave fully outside one
- If pre-training is the frontier again, the next-decade competitive moat may shift from "best on-chip algorithm" to **"best audio + audiogram + telemetry pre-training corpus"**
- Connects to existing wiki threads: closed-loop data flywheel, agentic engineering in hearing R&D, Autoresearch
