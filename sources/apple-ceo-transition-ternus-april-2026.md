# Apple CEO Transition — John Ternus

## Key Facts
- Tim Cook moves to Executive Chairman role
- John Ternus becomes Apple CEO (effective April 2026)
- Ternus previously led Apple's hardware engineering division

## Ternus's Hearing-Relevant Track Record
- Led development of **Apple Silicon** (M-series chips powering on-device ML)
- Oversaw **AirPods Pro** hardware program including:
  - FDA-cleared hearing aid features (OTC, mild-to-moderate)
  - Built-in hearing test (pure-tone audiometry via iPhone)
  - Adaptive Transparency mode
  - Heart rate monitoring (256 Hz PPG in AirPods Pro 3)
  - Active hearing protection
- Hardware engineering background signals continued investment in sensor-rich wearables

## Significance for Hearing Industry
- Ternus's elevation as CEO signals Apple's hardware-first, health-convergent strategy will continue or accelerate
- The executive who built AirPods Pro hearing features is now running the company
- Suggests Apple's hearing health push (OTC hearing aid, health monitoring, accessibility) is a strategic priority, not a side project
- Apple Silicon expertise means continued investment in on-device ML capable chips for wearables
