## Key Facts

- Oticon Intent launched February 2024 on the Sirius processing platform.
- DNN 2.0: trained on 12M+ sound scenes, a significant expansion over DNN 1.0 used in Oticon Real (2023).
- 4D sensor technology: accelerometer-based head movement detection and gesture control — infers listening intent from head-turning behavior to prioritize sound from the direction of interest.
- More Sound Intelligence 3.0 scene classification engine selects among 12M+ scene profiles to determine appropriate processing strategy in real time.
- 35% more speech cues delivered to the brain vs. predecessor (Oticon Real), per internal Eriksholm Research Centre studies.
- SoundScore rating: 4.9/5 from HearAdvisor independent evaluation.
- Positions Demant (parent company) as differentiating on cognitive science and brain-centered hearing strategy rather than raw DNN compute alone.
