# Advanced Bionics + Spiral Therapeutics Collaboration

## Key Extraction

- **Company:** Advanced Bionics (AB) — cochlear implant division of Sonova AG
- **Partner:** Spiral Therapeutics — precision drug delivery for inner ear
- **Announcement:** Equity investment + R&D collaboration to advance next-gen CI innovation
- **Date:** April 14, 2026
- **Details:**
  - AB made an equity investment in Spiral Therapeutics as part of Spiral's **$27M Series B** round
  - R&D collaboration to investigate drug delivery during cochlear implant surgery
  - Spiral's platform enables **minimally invasive, precise drug exposure to the cochlea**
  - Goal: address unmet clinical needs at the intersection of CI surgery and pharmacotherapy
  - AB is one of three major CI manufacturers globally (alongside Cochlear Ltd. and MED-EL)
- **Significance:**
  - First major CI manufacturer to invest in precision inner ear drug delivery — opens a new therapeutic axis for CI outcomes
  - Combining device (electrode array) + drug delivery is a convergence play: could improve residual hearing preservation during surgery, reduce inflammation, or enhance neural survival
  - Sonova investing in CI R&D while also pushing hearing aid AI (DEEPSONIC) — dual-track innovation strategy
  - CI technology has been relatively stagnant compared to hearing aid AI advances; this collaboration signals acceleration via pharmacology, not just electronics
  - Strategic for Sonova: CI is a growing segment ($2B+ global market) with higher margins than hearing aids
  - Research collaboration + equity investment model demonstrates conviction — not just a licensing deal
  - Potential long-term play: if drug delivery can protect or regenerate cochlear hair cells, it could expand CI candidacy criteria or improve outcomes for existing recipients
