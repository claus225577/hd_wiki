# Advanced Bionics Next-Gen CI Research Collaboration

## Key Extraction

- **Company:** Advanced Bionics (AB) — cochlear implant division of Sonova AG
- **Announcement:** New research collaboration to advance next-generation cochlear implant innovation
- **Date:** April 14, 2026
- **Details:**
  - Collaboration aimed at next-gen CI technology development
  - Specific research partners and technology focus areas not fully detailed in announcement
  - AB is one of three major CI manufacturers globally (alongside Cochlear Ltd. and MED-EL)
- **Significance:**
  - Sonova investing in CI R&D while also pushing hearing aid AI (DEEPSONIC) — dual-track innovation strategy
  - CI technology has been relatively stagnant compared to hearing aid AI advances; new collaboration signals potential acceleration
  - Next-gen CI could incorporate AI-driven sound coding (cf. AVSE-ECS research), personalized electrode mapping, or improved neural interface
  - Strategic for Sonova: CI is a growing segment ($2B+ global market) with higher margins than hearing aids
  - Research collaboration model (vs. internal-only R&D) suggests AB is seeking external expertise — potentially in AI, materials science, or neural engineering
