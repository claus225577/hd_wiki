# Advanced Bionics

Cochlear implant manufacturer and subsidiary of Sonova AG. One of three major CI manufacturers globally (alongside Cochlear Ltd. and MED-EL).

## Products

- **HiRes Ultra 3D** — Current cochlear implant platform
- **Naida CI** — Sound processor (external component)
- **HiRes sound coding** — Proprietary stimulation strategy

## Position Within Sonova

Advanced Bionics (AB) handles Sonova's cochlear implant business. While Phonak drives hearing aid innovation (DEEPSONIC), AB operates the CI R&D pipeline. The two divisions share some technology and research resources but develop distinct products for different clinical populations.

Sonova's dual-track strategy:
- **Hearing aids:** Phonak/Unitron — AI-driven sound processing (DEEPSONIC)
- **Cochlear implants:** Advanced Bionics — electrode array and sound coding innovation

## Research Collaboration (April 2026)

In April 2026, AB announced a new research collaboration to advance next-generation cochlear implant innovation. Key aspects:

- Focus on next-gen CI technology development
- Signals Sonova's continued investment in CI alongside hearing aid AI
- Potential areas: AI-driven sound coding, personalized electrode mapping, improved neural interface, advanced materials
- Research collaboration model (vs. internal-only R&D) suggests AB is seeking external expertise

## CI Market Context

- Global CI market: $2B+ annually, growing
- Three major players: Cochlear Ltd. (largest), MED-EL, Advanced Bionics
- CI technology has seen less AI innovation than hearing aids; AB collaboration may signal acceleration
- Higher margins than hearing aids; strategic growth segment for Sonova
