# Sonova AG

Swiss hearing care company and one of the world's largest hearing aid manufacturers. Headquartered in Stäfa, Switzerland.

## Brands
- **Phonak** — Premium hearing aid brand (Lumity, Infinio lines)
- **Unitron** — Mid-tier hearing aid brand
- **Advanced Bionics** — Cochlear implant division
- **AudioNova** — Retail hearing care network (acquired 2016)

## Technology Platform
- **PRISM chip** — Proprietary SoC powering Phonak Lumity and Infinio
- **AutoSense OS** — ML-based automatic program switching (acoustic scene classification)
- **Roger** — Proprietary wireless microphone system for noisy environments
- **myPhonak app** — Consumer-facing app with remote fitting capabilities

## Market Position
- Consistently #1 or #2 globally by revenue (competing with Demant)
- Strong in pediatric audiology
- Integrated manufacturer + retailer (AudioNova acquisition)

## Data Science Relevance
- AutoSense OS is one of the more sophisticated on-device ML systems in hearing aids
- Roger ecosystem generates usage data across education and workplace settings
- Remote fitting through myPhonak creates digital touchpoints for data collection
