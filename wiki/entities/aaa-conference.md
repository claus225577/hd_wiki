# American Academy of Audiology (AAA) Conference

The largest US audiology conference and the primary venue for hearing aid product launches, clinical research dissemination, and industry networking.

## AAA 2026

- **Theme:** "Amplify Your Value"
- **Dates:** April 22-25, 2026
- **Location:** San Antonio, Texas
- **Keynote:** Dr. Hassan Tetteh — "The Art of Human Care" — blending AI with empathy in healthcare
- **Scale:** 200+ sessions, 130+ exhibitors, 16 tracks
- **New track:** "Amplify Your Value" track
- **HearTECH Hub** — dedicated technology showcase area within the expo hall
- **Oticon Foundation:** $30K donation to AAA Foundation for student scholarships

### Major Exhibitors (2026)
Oticon, Starkey, Phonak, ReSound, Signia, Cochlear — all Big 6 represented (130+ exhibitors total).

### Notable Launches at AAA 2026
- **Oticon Zeal** — rechargeable ITE with DNN 2.0 on Sirius chip, Auracast, 20-hour battery, tap controls (conference debut)
- **Starkey Omega AI** — showcasing Auracast, AI-driven sound processing, health/wellness integration
- **Oticon Verit** and **Play SI** showcased (launched earlier in April 2026)
- **WS Audiology Sound Preference Program** — global rollout initiated Apr 20, showcased at AAA
- Additional announcements from other exhibitors

### Industry Leaders Panel (May 2026 coverage)
A panel of executives from **Sonova, Demant North America, GN/ReSound, Starkey, and Cochlear America** identified the two defining issues of the next 5 years:
1. **AI integration is now table-stakes** — no longer differentiating, expected as a baseline.
2. **Audiologist workforce shortage is structural** — graduations not keeping pace with retirements; the industry's most pressing operational risk.

Panel consensus: reframe hearing loss as a **core health issue** (cognition, falls, isolation), not lifestyle. AI tools (auto-fitting, remote monitoring, decision support) framed as a partial response to fewer audiologists per patient.

### Significance of 2026 Theme
"Amplify Your Value" reflects the audiology profession's response to OTC disruption and consumer tech convergence (AirPods hearing features, etc.). The message to audiologists: demonstrate irreplaceable expertise beyond device dispensing. Dr. Tetteh's keynote on blending AI with empathy signals the industry's awareness that AI adoption must be paired with human-centered care — not AI replacement of audiologists, but AI augmentation of their value. Oticon's $30K scholarship donation reflects manufacturer investment in the audiology workforce pipeline amid ongoing shortage concerns (see [[audiologist-workforce-shortage]]).

## Role in the Hearing + AI Landscape

- Primary industry venue for hearing aid AI product launches
- Increasingly features AI/ML research sessions alongside traditional clinical presentations
- HearTECH Hub creates dedicated space for technology demonstrations
- Conference presentations influence clinical adoption of AI features
- AAA attendance by all Big 6 manufacturers makes it the de facto annual industry benchmark
