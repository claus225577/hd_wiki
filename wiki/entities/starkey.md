# Starkey

American hearing aid manufacturer, headquartered in Eden Prairie, Minnesota. The only major hearing aid manufacturer still based in the US.

## Products
- **Genesis AI** — Current flagship platform
- **Evolv AI** — Previous generation

## Technology
- **Edge AI** — Starkey's brand for on-device ML capabilities
- **Edge Mode** — On-demand environmental optimization
- **Health monitoring** — Fall detection, heart rate (via in-ear sensors), activity tracking
- **Thrive app** — Consumer app integrating hearing + health data

## Strategic Position
- Positioned as a "health tech" company, not just hearing aids
- Most aggressive push into health monitoring features beyond hearing
- Privately held (unlike publicly traded competitors)
- Strong philanthropic brand (Starkey Hearing Foundation)

## Data Science Relevance
- Furthest along in multi-modal health sensing in hearing aids
- Fall detection uses accelerometer + ML classification on-device
- Health data integration creates potential for hearing aids as health platforms
- Edge Mode is an interesting UX for user-triggered ML optimization
