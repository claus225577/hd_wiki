# Amplifon

World's largest hearing aid retailer. Headquartered in Milan, Italy. Undergoing a major strategic transformation through the acquisition of GN Hearing (ReSound).

## Scale (Post-Acquisition)
- **20,000+ employees** combined (pre-acquisition: ~18,000)
- **10,000+ clinics** worldwide
- **100+ countries** of operation
- **€3.3 billion combined revenue** (Amplifon retail + GN Hearing manufacturing)
- **US becomes the biggest single market** in the combined entity

## Pre-Acquisition Business Model
- Pure retail/distribution — historically not a manufacturer
- Sources hearing aids from multiple manufacturers (Sonova, Demant, etc.)
- Revenue from device sales + fitting services + aftercare
- Largest single dataset of hearing aid patient journeys globally

## Strategic Shift: GN Hearing Acquisition (2026)

### Deal Terms
- **Total deal value: ~€2.3 billion (~$2.64 billion USD)**
- GN Group receives: **€1.69 billion cash + 56 million Amplifon shares**
- Expected close: **end of 2026** (subject to regulatory/antitrust approval)

### What Changes
- Transforms Amplifon from pure retailer to **vertically integrated manufacturer + retailer**
- Amplifon gains ownership of ReSound (AI hearing aids, including Vivia with dedicated DNN chip) and Beltone
- Joins Sonova (AudioNova) and Demant (HearingLife) as the third major vertically integrated player

### Synergies
- **€60–80 million in annual synergies projected by 2029**
- Synergies from: manufacturing cost reductions, cross-selling proprietary devices through clinics, shared R&D/data infrastructure

### Data Science Significance
Post-merger, Amplifon creates a unique closed-loop data architecture:
- **Patient journey data** (clinic visits, audiograms, fitting outcomes, returns, satisfaction) — Amplifon's existing strength
- **Product telemetry** (usage patterns, program switching, environment exposure) — GN Hearing's device data
- Combined under one roof: potential to correlate fitting decisions → product performance → patient outcomes at scale
- Largest integrated hearing health dataset globally once merged

## Competitive Context
The acquisition directly mirrors vertical integration strategies already executed by:
- **Sonova**: owns Phonak (manufacturer) + AudioNova (retail)
- **Demant**: owns Oticon (manufacturer) + HearingLife (retail)

Amplifon was the last major pure retailer; this move closes that gap and adds a manufacturing capability Sonova and Demant have had for years.
