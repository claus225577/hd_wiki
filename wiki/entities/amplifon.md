# Amplifon

World's largest hearing aid retailer. Headquartered in Milan, Italy.

## Scale
- 10,000+ clinics worldwide
- Operations in 100+ countries
- ~18,000 employees

## Business Model
- Pure retail/distribution — historically not a manufacturer
- Sources hearing aids from multiple manufacturers (Sonova, Demant, etc.)
- Revenue from device sales + fitting services + aftercare

## Strategic Shift: GN Hearing Acquisition (2026)
- Acquired GN Hearing (ReSound) for ~$2.6B
- Transforms from pure retailer to vertically integrated retailer + manufacturer
- Creates unique data position: patient journey data + product telemetry under one roof

## Data Science Significance
- Largest single dataset of hearing aid patient journeys globally
- Post-merger: closed-loop data flywheel connecting retail outcomes to product R&D
- Potential for personalization at scale: which device + fitting → best outcomes for which patient profiles
