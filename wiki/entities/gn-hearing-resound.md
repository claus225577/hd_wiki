# GN Hearing / ReSound

Danish hearing aid manufacturer, formerly part of GN Group (which also owns Jabra). Acquired by Amplifon in 2026 for ~$2.6B.

## Brands & Products
- **ReSound** — Primary hearing aid brand
- **ReSound Nexia** — First hearing aid with Auracast support
- **ReSound Vivia** — Rechargeable platform with Auracast
- **Beltone** — Secondary brand (primarily North American market)

## Technology
- **Organic Hearing platform** — Proprietary chip and signal processing
- **M&RIE (Microphone & Receiver-in-Ear)** — Unique receiver design using ear's natural shape
- **Auracast pioneer** — First to market with Bluetooth LE Audio broadcast support
- **ReSound Smart 3D app** — Consumer app with hearing aid controls

## Strategic Position
- Acquired by Amplifon (2026), ending independence
- Was part of GN Group alongside Jabra (enterprise audio)
- Cross-pollination between Jabra and ReSound R&D teams was a noted advantage
