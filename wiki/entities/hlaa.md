# Hearing Loss Association of America (HLAA)

The largest US consumer-advocacy organization for people with hearing loss. The canonical patient-voice counterpart to the engineering/clinical thread in hearing care.

## What HLAA Is

- US-based 501(c)(3) consumer organization, founded 1979 (then SHHH — Self Help for Hard of Hearing People).
- Membership-driven: people with hearing loss, family members, hearing-care professionals as allies.
- Network of state chapters and local support groups; combined membership + chapter-network reach is the largest patient-side platform in US hearing-care policy.

## Annual Convention

The flagship event each year — three days, ~1,500-2,500 attendees, audiologists + manufacturers + end-users in the same rooms.

| Year | Edition | Venue | AI Symposium |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2026 | 41st | Galt House, Louisville KY (June 10-12) | Yes — "The AI Revolution in Hearing Health: Smarter Tech, Greater Access" (NIDCD-supported, Fri 12 Jun, 10am CT) |

Distinguishing feature: **the convention is itself an accessibility deployment** — real-time captioning for every session, hearing loops in every meeting room. The largest annual real-world stress test of the consumer accessibility stack each year. See [[../sources/hlaa-2026-convention-louisville-june-2026]] for 2026 detail.

## Why It Matters for Hearing AI

### Patient-voice rung of the discourse ladder
The three rungs of the 2026 discourse:

| Rung | Anchor 2026 venue | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| Engineering | Interspeech 2026 + arXiv flow | ML researchers, chip engineers |
| Clinical | HEAL 2026 Padua (4-6 June), WCA 2026 Seoul (May) | Audiologists, clinician-scientists |
| **Patient** | **HLAA 2026 Louisville (10-12 June)** | **End-users, advocates, policy** |

What HLAA's membership frames as "delivered AI value" vs "AI hype" gets translated into congressional outreach within weeks. See [[../concepts/patient-advocacy-hearing-health]].

### Manufacturer feedback loop
The convention expo is one of the few venues where manufacturers preview features to a community that will publicly evaluate them within days. This is the closest the industry has to a [[../concepts/closed-loop-data-flywheel]] at the *policy-perception* layer, distinct from the device-telemetry layer.

### Legislative weight
HLAA is named as an endorsing organization on both the House and Senate versions of the Veterans Hearing Aid Improvement Act push. Their member base calls Congress.

### Accessibility-substrate deployment
- Every meeting room hearing loop = consumer-scale T-coil / Auracast interop test
- Every session captioned = consumer-scale real-time captioning stress test

See [[../concepts/auracast-bluetooth-le-audio]] and [[../concepts/visual-substitution-hearing-accessibility]] for the technology threads being tested at HLAA scale.
