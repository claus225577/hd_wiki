# Orka (& Bose Partnership)

Orka is a hearing technology company that debuted the world's first RIC hearing aid with active noise cancellation at AWE 2026, powered by Bose ANC technology.

## Orka O1 Pro (April 2026)

- **World's first RIC hearing aid with ANC** — combines prescription-grade amplification with Bose QuietControl active noise cancellation
- **Bose SpeechClarity** — dedicated speech enhancement feature from Bose
- **Weight:** 3.5 grams
- **Battery life:** 35+ hours (exceptional given ANC power overhead)
- **Debuted at:** AWE (Augmented World Expo) 2026

## Technical Significance

### Dual-Layer Noise Management
The O1 Pro introduces a new paradigm: ANC + DNN speech enhancement as complementary layers:
1. **ANC layer (Bose QuietControl):** Physical noise attenuation — reduces broadband environmental noise before it reaches the microphone
2. **Speech enhancement layer:** Computational separation of speech from residual noise after ANC

This dual-layer approach could free DNN capacity for finer speech enhancement by offloading broadband noise cancellation to the ANC system.

### Consumer-Medical Bridge
- Bose's partnership with Orka bridges consumer audio technology (where Bose excels at ANC) with medical hearing amplification
- Debuting at AWE rather than AAA signals positioning as a tech/consumer brand rather than traditional audiology
- Follows Bose's earlier entry into (and partial exit from) OTC hearing aids — this time partnering rather than going direct

## Competitive Implications

- Puts pressure on Big 6 manufacturers to integrate ANC or partner with consumer audio companies
- ANC has been available in consumer earbuds (AirPods Pro, Sony WF-1000XM) but not in hearing aids — this closes that gap
- The 3.5g weight and 35hr battery life set new benchmarks for feature-rich hearing aids
