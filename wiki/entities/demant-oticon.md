# Demant / Oticon

Danish hearing care conglomerate. One of the top 2-3 hearing aid companies globally.

## Brands
- **Oticon** — Premium hearing aid brand (Real, Intent lines)
- **Bernafon** — Mid-tier brand
- **Sonic** — Value brand
- **Oticon Medical** — Bone-anchored hearing systems
- **HearingLife** — Retail hearing care network

## Technology
- **Polaris platform** — Proprietary SoC with dedicated DNN accelerator
- **BrainHearing** — Approach focused on how the brain processes sound
- **DNN (Deep Neural Network) noise reduction** — Trained on 12M+ real-world sound scenes
- **Oticon Intent** — Uses motion and hearing intent sensors

## Market Position
- #1 or #2 globally (neck and neck with Sonova)
- Vertically integrated (manufacturer + HearingLife retail)
- Strong research tradition (Eriksholm Research Centre)

## Data Science Relevance
- Polaris chip has dedicated neural network accelerator — one of first in hearing aids
- Oticon Intent uses 4D sensor fusion (motion + sound) for intent detection
- DNN trained on 12M+ sound scenes is one of the largest hearing-specific training datasets
