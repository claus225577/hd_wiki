# WS Audiology / Signia

Formed in 2019 from the merger of Sivantos (Siemens hearing) and Widex. Headquartered in Singapore.

## Brands
- **Signia** — Premium brand (formerly Siemens)
- **Widex** — Premium brand known for sound quality
- **Rexton** — Mid-tier brand
- **Audio Service** — European brand

## Technology
- **TwinLink** — Dual-radio platform (Bluetooth Classic + proprietary near-field)
- **Signia IX** — Active Sound Processing with conversation enhancement
- **Widex MOMENT** — Known for ultra-low latency processing (PureSound)
- **Own Voice Processing (OVP)** — Separates wearer's own voice from environmental sound

## Notable
- Widex historically known for superior sound quality and music processing
- Signia's Active Scanning technology processes 15M data points per hour
- OVP is a unique ML problem: classify and separately process the wearer's own voice in real-time
